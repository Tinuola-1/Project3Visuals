Real Estate Market Hotness

Explore market trends around the country. What are the most expensive markets in the country? Which places have seen list prices appreciate the fastest? How fast is inventory selling in the different markets around the country? How does seasonality impact market movement across the nation? Obtain insights on what makes real estate local.

How it's Determined:

The Realtor.com Market Hotness Index exposes how local areas are experiencing fast moving supply and rising demand. Using their proprietary insights on buyer activity and the most comprehensive data on active inventory, the analysis breaks down demand and supply dynamics to rank metro areas, counties and zip codes relative to the rest of the country. Realtor.com examines listing views by market as an indicator of demand and median days on market as an indicator of supply.
